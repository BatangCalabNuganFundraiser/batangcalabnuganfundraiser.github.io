The Bata ng Calabnugan, Inc NGO was founded in 2005, by Italian national Francesco Izzo and his wife Flora Aguit, who is from the Philippines, along with a host of volunteers with the idea of providing a safe and caring environment for orphaned and at-risk girls in the Philippines. The girls come from a variety of backgrounds including those who have been orphaned, were living on the street, have experienced physical or sexual abuse, or have mental or physical disabilities. We provide:
  • A comfortable family-style living environment with lots of love and individual attention
  • Healthy meals that include our own organic eggs and homegrown fruits and vegetables.
  • Education from kindergarten through college. Since we know how important education is for our girls futures we choose to send them to private school to maximize their learning potential.
  • Immunizations and medical care.
  • Extra curricular activities such as hiking, swimming, sports, camping and travel help give the girls a well-rounded life experience.
The annual cost of the orphanage in providing adequate nutrition is approximately one million pesos (approx $20,000).
The dream of this ride is to guarantee daily food and meals for the shelter for 3 years.
